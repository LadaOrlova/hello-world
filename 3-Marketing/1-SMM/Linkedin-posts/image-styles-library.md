# Image Styles Library for LinkedIn Posts

When you find a style you love, mark it with a star in the **Status** field.
The linkedin-post skill reads styles from this file.

**Key principles for ALL styles:**
- Every image must radiate positive energy — hopeful, joyful, uplifting
- Professional editorial quality that stops the scroll on LinkedIn
- Clear visual metaphor readable even at small mobile size
- Characters (when present) look confident, happy, energized
- No dark, dystopian, gloomy, or stressful imagery
- No text, captions, speech bubbles, or words in the image

---

## Style 1: Warm Ink & Watercolor Editorial (PROVEN)

**Status:** star
**ID:** warm-ink-watercolor

Editorial illustration with hand-drawn ink pen lines and warm watercolor washes. Muted earth-tone palette — warm beige, golden sand, soft brown — with one bright orange accent color that draws the eye. Confident loose pen strokes, not too detailed. Characters have friendly, slightly stylized proportions with expressive faces and open body language. Background washes bleed softly into white paper. Large arched windows or open spaces let natural light flood the scene. The overall feeling is like a favorite page from a beautifully illustrated book — warm, inviting, full of gentle optimism. Composition leaves breathing room, never cluttered.

**Proven on:** 2026-02-20 (PM Hypothesis Factory post) — generated excellent result with Gemini 3 Pro.

## Style 2: New Yorker Cartoon Classic

**Status:** star
**ID:** new-yorker-classic

Editorial illustration in the style of classic New Yorker magazine covers by artists like Sempé and Steinberg. Precise ink pen lines with selective cross-hatching for shadow and depth. Subtle watercolor washes in a refined palette — slate blue, warm grey, ivory, with one pop of coral or golden yellow. Sophisticated composition with generous white space and one strong focal point. Witty visual metaphor that rewards a second look. Characters are elegantly simplified — expressive through posture and gesture, not detail. The humor is gentle and warm, never cynical. A sense of quiet confidence and intellectual charm. Aspect ratio works well as a wide editorial banner.

**Note:** This is the default style for Pass 1 (metaphor discovery). All 10 metaphor variants are generated in this style first.

## Style 3: Soft Gradient Editorial

**Status:** available
**ID:** soft-gradient

Elegant digital illustration with smooth, dreamy gradients and soft rounded forms. Palette: warm peach flowing into soft lavender, golden hour amber into rose pink, with touches of mint and cream. Gentle shadows create depth without harshness. Characters have friendly rounded proportions and calm, confident expressions. Soft light sources create a warm glow effect on key elements. Clean vector shapes with subtle grain texture overlay for warmth. Generous negative space filled with delicate gradient washes. Inspired by the best of Headspace and Calm app aesthetics, but with more editorial edge. Soothing, premium, trustworthy. Like a deep breath on a warm evening.

## Style 4: Nordic Light

**Status:** available
**ID:** nordic-light

Scandinavian-inspired editorial illustration radiating hygge warmth. Simple geometric forms — circles, soft rectangles, organic curves — that feel hand-placed and intentional. Muted but warm palette: natural sand, soft terracotta, forest sage green, warm grey-blue, plenty of cream white. At least 40% white space. One clear focal element with minimal supporting shapes. Subtle paper texture visible throughout. Thin, precise lines where needed. Inspired by Marimekko patterns and Finnish design philosophy — nothing unnecessary, everything beautiful. Small delightful details reward closer inspection. The visual warmth of a sunlit Nordic studio. Calm, grounding, quietly joyful.

---

## Style 5: Golden Hour Vintage

**Status:** available
**ID:** golden-hour

Retro-warm illustration bathed in golden hour light. Rich sunset palette — warm amber, dusty rose, terracotta, soft cream, muted olive. Everything glows with the warmth of late afternoon sun. Rounded organic shapes and flowing curves with slight film-grain texture. Inspired by vintage travel posters, 1970s album art, and golden-age advertising illustration. Bold simplified silhouettes with layered translucent color planes. Visible sunbeams or warm light rays as compositional elements. Characters appear in confident, optimistic poses — looking forward, reaching upward, moving with purpose. Nostalgic but not dated — timeless warmth with modern sensibility. The feeling of possibility at the start of something great.

# Decision Log — PR Draft: Product Strategy for StartupNation

## [STAGE 0.4] Word budget check
**Context:** 5 theses at varying depth, need to fit 800-1500 words
**Options:** Tight budget (800), comfortable (1200), generous (1500)
**Decision:** Target ~1300 words (comfortable fit for 5 theses)
**Rationale:** 5 theses × ~200 avg + 120 hook + 130 close = ~1250. Leaves room for cases.
**Trade-off:** Some theses will get lighter treatment (~150 words vs ~300)

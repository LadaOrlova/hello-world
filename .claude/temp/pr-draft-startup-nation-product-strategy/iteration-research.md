### TASK 2: Find a direct link for the InVideo JTBD pivot story
**Found:** Yes
**URL:** https://therewiredgroup.com/case-studies/invideo-jtbd/
**Alternative URL (SaaS Club podcast w/ Bob Moesta):** https://saasclub.io/podcast/jobs-to-be-done-bob-moesta-423/
**Additional URL (Re-Wired article):** https://therewiredgroup.com/learn/5-ways-to-design-launch-and-scale-your-saas-product/

**Key data/quote:**
- InVideo plateaued at 20% YoY growth despite healthy sign-ups
- JTBD research (10 weeks with Re-Wired Group) revealed 3 jobs pulling product in different directions
- Advanced users requested complex features but churned — CEO Sanket Shah: "We were building for a group that kept asking for more but would ultimately leave"
- Strategic decision: dropped 3rd job (advanced users), focused on 2 jobs (beginners + small businesses needing video)
- Results within 6 months: $25M in new revenue, 20M active users, 190+ countries, became the 33rd most-used AI software globally (per A16Z)
- Full pivot to AI video creation → eventually reached $70M ARR within 18 months

**Suggested text (for the article):**
«InVideo три года пытались угодить всем трём сегментам — рост застрял на 20% в год. JTBD-исследование с командой Bob Moesta (Re-Wired Group) показало: продвинутые видеографы просили сложные фичи, но быстро уходили. CEO Sanket Shah принял решение отказаться от этого сегмента и сфокусировался на двух остальных jobs. За шесть месяцев — $25 млн новой выручки, 20 миллионов пользователей, 190+ стран.»

### TASK 3: Find a DIFFERENT case for mechanic #2 (Up-market shift)
**Found:** Yes — **Freshworks (FRSH)**
**URL:** https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/freshworks-q4-2025-presentation-first-profitable-quarter-amid-slowing-growth-93CH-4498468
**Additional URL:** https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/freshworks-q3-2025-slides-revenue-hits-2151m-amid-enterprise-customer-shift-93CH-4335962

**Key data/quote:**
- Freshworks started as cheap helpdesk for SMBs (Freshdesk), competing with Zendesk on price
- Deliberately shifted upmarket to enterprise over 2024–2025
- Now 60%+ of total ARR comes from mid-market and enterprise customers
- Customers paying $50K+/year ARR: 3,760 — growing 23% YoY (while total customer count grew only 3%)
- Net Dollar Retention improved from 103% to 108% — showing expansion within existing accounts
- Q4 2025: first GAAP-profitable quarter ($39.7M operating income, 18% margin)
- Revenue: $222.7M/quarter ($838.8M full year 2025)
- Employee Experience (EX) business: 75%+ of ARR from mid-market/enterprise, ARR grew 33% YoY

**ARGUMENT TEST:** "The up-market shift mechanic changes the game. For example, Freshworks started as a cheap Zendesk alternative for small businesses. When they shifted focus to enterprise — customers paying $50K+/year grew 23% YoY while total customers grew only 3%. Result: first profitable quarter in company history, $840M annual revenue, 60%+ ARR from enterprise." = PROOF. Passes test.

**Suggested text (for the article):**
«Freshworks начинал как дешёвая альтернатива Zendesk для малого бизнеса. Осознанный сдвиг в enterprise привёл к тому, что клиенты с чеком $50K+/год выросли на 23% за год, хотя общее число клиентов выросло лишь на 3%. Результат: первый прибыльный квартал в истории компании, $840M годовой выручки, более 60% ARR — от enterprise-сегмента.»

### TASK 4: Find a better case for mechanic #4 (Capture the next job / cross-sell)
**Found:** Yes — **Toast (TOST)** — restaurant POS platform
**URL:** https://www.fool.com/investing/2026/02/03/this-restaurant-focused-fintech-has-a-recurring-re/
**Additional data sources:** Motley Fool, Investing.com, Toast earnings

**Key data/quote:**
- Toast started as restaurant POS system. Then identified "next jobs" restaurants need: payroll, lending, marketing, insurance
- Restaurants using Toast Payroll use 2x more software products from Toast and pay $4,000–$5,000 MORE per year
- Toast Capital originated $1B+ in loans in 2024
- ARR crossed $2B by Q3 2025 (30% YoY growth)
- Net revenue retention rate: 110% (FY2024) — proving cross-sell drives expansion
- 156,000 restaurant locations served (of 1.4M total addressable market)
- Toast IQ and Toast Advertising = new adjacent products for marketing/insights
- Payroll data enables Toast to offer health insurance at better terms than traditional insurers

**ARGUMENT TEST:** "Capture the next job changes the game. For example, Toast started as a restaurant POS. They identified the next jobs — payroll, lending, marketing. Restaurants using Toast Payroll buy 2x more products and pay $4K–$5K more per year. Toast Capital originated over $1B in loans. ARR crossed $2B." = PROOF. Passes test.

**Suggested text (for the article):**
«Toast начинали как POS-система для ресторанов. Но вместо остановки они спросили: что ещё нужно ресторатору? Ответ: зарплата, кредиты, маркетинг, страховка. Рестораны, подключившие Toast Payroll, используют вдвое больше продуктов Toast и платят на $4–5 тысяч в год больше. Toast Capital выдал более $1 млрд кредитов за 2024 год. ARR превысил $2 млрд — рост 30% в год.»

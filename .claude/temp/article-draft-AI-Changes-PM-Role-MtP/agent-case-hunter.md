### Candidate 1: Spotify "Honk" -- Engineers Haven't Written Code Since December (RECOMMENDED)

**What happened:** On Spotify's Q4 2025 earnings call (February 10, 2026), Soderstrohm dropped a bombshell: "Our best developers have not written a single line of code since December." **Why it works for the hook:** A globally known consumer brand. The reader pictures a developer on a train, fixing bugs via chat. Concrete, visual, immediate. And the punchline: the best developers code ZERO lines -- they judge, review, decide.

## THESIS A6: K-Shaped Bifurcation of PM Market (Builders vs Coordinators)

### Case 6.1: Spotify vs. METR -- Same Technology, Opposite Results (2025-2026) -- NEW (COMPOSITE)

**Who:** Spotify (Honk system) vs. METR study participants
**What happened:** Spotify's best developers haven't coded since December, shipping 50+ features with AI. METR's experienced OSS developers were 19% slower with the same class of AI tools. Same technology, opposite results. The difference: organizational architecture, infrastructure (Honk), and role redesign. This IS the K-shape: companies that restructure around AI (builders) surge; those that bolt AI onto existing workflows (coordinators) stall or regress.
**Numbers:** Spotify: 50+ features, 0 lines coded; METR: 19% slower
**Thesis:** A6

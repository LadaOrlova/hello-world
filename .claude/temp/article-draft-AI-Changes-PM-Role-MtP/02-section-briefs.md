## STYLE GUIDE (ключевые правила из agent-style-researcher)

**Title:** Sentence case. Contrarian claim + qualifier. 8-14 words. Avoid "AI is transforming PM."
**Hook:** Open with TENSION or PARADOX. Never "AI is transforming everything."
**Paragraphs:** 3-4 sentences (60-100 words). Never >120 words. One idea per paragraph.
**Subheadings:** Every 200-400 words. Sentence case. Scannable outline on their own. 5-7 for ~1500 words.
**Tone:** 5-6/10 formality. Peer practitioner. I-You-We rotation.
**Expert quotes:** 2-3 per article. One sentence each, attributed inline. Recognize PM names.
**Data:** 2-4 data points. At least one in first 300 words. Weave into prose.
**Cases:** 2-3 brief (1-3 sentence) company examples. Show specific failure/surprise.
**Conclusion:** One-sentence synthesis + forward-looking imperative. Quotable last sentence.
**Actionable:** Every major section should end with something the reader can DO Monday.
**Lists:** Only for actionable advice/frameworks. Narrative stays in prose. 3-6 items.
**PROHIBITED:** "AI is transforming everything", academic tone, paragraphs >5 sentences, "the future is bright" conclusions, passive voice, block quotes >2 sentences.

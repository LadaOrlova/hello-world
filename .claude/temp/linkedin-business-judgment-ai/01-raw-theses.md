# Raw Theses — LinkedIn Post: Business Judgment in the AI Era

## T1: Business Judgment is the #1 PM skill in the AI era
When AI automates execution, the quality of decisions — which hypotheses to test, which markets to choose, which trade-offs to make — becomes the key differentiator. PMs who just "move tasks in a task tracker" will be replaced by AI agents.

## T2: Judgment has a precise definition via the AURA causal chain
It's the ability to diagnose WHERE in the chain (Market → Segments → Jobs → Value → Communication → Unit Economics → Profit) the problem is, and make the RIGHT strategic choice. Not intuition — a trainable diagnostic skill.

## T3: The RAT formula proves it mathematically
When AI crashes the cost of testing (denominator), the numerator dominates: risk identification and consequence evaluation. Speed of doing becomes cheap; speed of THINKING correctly becomes priceless.

## T4: PM theater is dead
Cagan (SVPG, 2026) confirms AI makes PRD/roadmap/user stories cheap and replaceable by agents. PM value shifts from artifact production to accountability for outcomes.

## T5: The anxiety is real — and the reframe is psychological safety
67% of PMs report anxiety about AI (Perspective AI). But Infosys+MIT (2025): 83% of leaders say psychological safety measurably impacts AI initiative success. The hesitation is "grounded in ambiguity, not ego" (HBR).

## T6: Vibe coding without judgment = "cursed feature cycle" on steroids
AI accelerates execution, but if direction is wrong, speed only increases losses. The brain acts as an investor of metabolic resources; AI cannot evaluate the subjective PAIN of the customer — judgment develops through practice, not prompts.

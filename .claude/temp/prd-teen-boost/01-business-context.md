# Business Context

## Batch 1: Business & Monetization
- **Business goal:** Launch a new product for a new segment (teens) + new market (international EN-speaking)
- **Product type:** Online educational intensive (1-week bootcamp)
- **Monetization:** One-time payment ($299-$499 US market, 19,900-29,900 RUB Russian market)
- **Constraints:** Small team (2-4 people), vibe-coding approach to development, leveraging existing BOOST curriculum

## Batch 2: Context & Channels
- **Data / traction:** 4 successful cohorts of adult BOOST (120+ students each, NPS 9+/10), extensive student feedback, proven curriculum
- **Horizon:** < 2 weeks to MVP launch
- **Channels:** Paid ads (Meta, Google), community/word-of-mouth, parenting forums, school partnerships
- **Competitors (known):** iD Tech, AI Camp, Code Ninjas, Create & Learn, Juni Learning (teen coding); no known competitors in "vibe coding for teens" specifically

## AURA Recommendations

### Variant A: "Prove demand in a new niche"
Validate that parents in US/EU are willing to pay $349-$499 for a 1-week AI vibe-coding intensive for their teens (13-17). Success metric: 30 paid enrollments in the first cohort within 4 weeks of launch. Focus on the "future-proof my child" parent segment.

### Variant B: "Own the vibe-coding-for-teens category"
Position as the FIRST and ONLY program teaching teens to build real products with AI coding tools (not traditional coding). Create a new category before competitors catch on. Success metric: 50 enrollments across EN + RU markets in 6 weeks.

### Variant C: "Build a global teen AI academy" (most ambitious)
Launch Teen Boost as the flagship product of a recurring teen AI education brand. First cohort proves the model, then scale to monthly cohorts with retention (advanced levels, alumni community). Target: 100 students in first 2 months, $50K+ revenue.

**Selected: Variant B** — "Own the vibe-coding-for-teens category." This is the most strategically valuable because:
1. No direct competitors in this exact niche yet
2. Leverages existing proven BOOST curriculum (low cost to adapt)
3. Creates category ownership which is the strongest competitive moat
4. International market multiplies the TAM significantly

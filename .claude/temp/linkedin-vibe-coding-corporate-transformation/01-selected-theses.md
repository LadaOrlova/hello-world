# Selected Theses for LinkedIn Post

## T3: Historical Parallel — Product Transformation
This is exactly how the product management revolution happened. Someone inside a company learned about product thinking, came back and said "let's do this properly." Enough of those people created a tipping point. The same thing is about to happen with AI agents and vibe-coding. Bottom-up transformation driven by individuals who've tasted a better way of working.

## T4: The Playbook for the Awakened Employee
Go to your boss and say: "Want to see something insane? Give me a zone of ownership and autonomy. We'll ship at 10x speed, and you'll take the credit upstairs." Leadership will be stunned by the output. This is how people become leaders: show results → capture more resources. It's a concrete, actionable playbook for corporate employees who've discovered vibe-coding.

## T7: Counter — "Vibe-Coders Will Burn Out in 2 Months"
Real risk. You need routines, anchors, balance. But it's better to burn bright on a rocket than to slowly smolder in meetings. Acknowledge the counterargument honestly but reframe it — the alternative (staying slow) is its own form of burnout.
